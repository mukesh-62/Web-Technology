Maggi Chicken Cup

About Maggi Chicken Cup

Maggi Chicken Cup is a convenient and tasty option for a quick meal or snack. Packed with the delicious flavor of chicken, this cup noodle variant is a favorite among many.

Whether you're at home, at work, or on the go, Maggi Chicken Cup provides a satisfying and flavorful experience that's ready in minutes.

Preparation Instructions

  1. Open the lid partially and add boiling water to the fill line inside the cup.
  2. Close the lid and let it sit for 3 minutes.
  3. Stir well and enjoy your hot and delicious Maggi Chicken Cup!
