How to Cook Maggi 2 Minute Noodles

Cooking Maggi 2 Minute Noodles is quick and easy:

  1. Boil water in a pot.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles and the flavoring masala to the boiling water.
  3. Stir occasionally and cook for about 2 minutes or until the noodles are soft.
  4. Remove from heat and serve hot.
