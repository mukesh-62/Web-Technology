Maggi Veg Masala Noodles

About Maggi Veg Masala Noodles

Maggi Veg Masala Noodles are a delicious vegetarian variant of the popular Maggi 2 Minute Noodles. Packed with the goodness of vegetables and aromatic spices, these noodles offer a flavorful and satisfying meal option.

Whether you're a vegetarian or simply looking for a tasty noodle dish, Maggi Veg Masala Noodles are sure to please your taste buds.

Ingredients

The key ingredients in Maggi Veg Masala Noodles include:

  • Noodles
  • Vegetable Seasoning Mix
  • Spices
  • Vegetables (such as peas, carrots, and beans)

How to Cook Maggi Veg Masala Noodles

Preparing Maggi Veg Masala Noodles is quick and easy:

  1. Boil 1Â½ cups of water in a pot.
  2. Add the Maggi noodles and the vegetable seasoning mix to the boiling water.
  3. Stir occasionally and cook for about 2 minutes or until the noodles are soft.
  4. Add chopped vegetables of your choice and continue cooking for another minute.
  5. Remove from heat and serve hot.
