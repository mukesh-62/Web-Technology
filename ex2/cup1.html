Maggi Masala Cup

About Maggi Masala Cup

Maggi Masala Cup is a convenient and delicious instant noodle option for people on the go. Whether you're at work, school, or traveling, Maggi Masala Cup offers a quick and satisfying meal solution.

Each cup contains pre-cooked noodles and a flavorful masala mix, allowing you to enjoy the authentic taste of Maggi noodles in just a few minutes.

How to Prepare Maggi Masala Cup

Preparing Maggi Masala Cup is quick and easy:

  1. Open the cup and remove the masala sachet.
  2. Fill the cup with boiling water up to the indicated fill line.
  3. Close the lid and let it sit for 2-3 minutes.
  4. Open the lid, add the masala mix, and stir well.
  5. Your delicious Maggi Masala Cup is ready to enjoy!
